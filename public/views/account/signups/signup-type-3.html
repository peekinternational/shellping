Parent/sibling/friend of Member Signup

As a parent/sibling/friend of the member of the group but not the person that runs it you have two options:

  1. **RECOMMENDED** Create your group on Shelping.COM. This involves you putting up the groups name and a brief description so it's not a lot of work. Once you have created your group and your account you are provided with a special link that you can send to the person who actually runs your group. That person can then create their own account on Shelping.COM and take ownership of your group. The point of you creating the group first is that it gives you more to share to the other members and also you and the other members and their friends and families can immediately start helping the group through changing brand to Shelping.COM. To start by creating your group click click here.
  2. Just create your account on Shelping.COM and not worry about creating the group yet as you can add a group at any time. Once you have your Shelping.COM account, share Shelping.COM on your social media and let the person who runs the group know to come and create the group on Shelping.COM. Once the group is created you and all the other members and their friends and families can select it to be the group you support by changing your brand to Shelping.COM. In the meantime you can still go ahead and buy Shleping.COM products or create Shelping.COM subscriptions that you can build up credit from meaning as soon as your group is created by the person who runs it you already have credit you can give to support your group. To go ahead and join Shelping.COM click here.
